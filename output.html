# Marathon Training Nutrition Plan

## Daily Calorie Target: 3000-3500 calories

### Macronutrient Breakdown
- **Carbohydrates**: 55-60% (410-525g) — fuel for endurance
- **Protein**: 15-20% (112-175g) — muscle recovery
- **Fat**: 20-25% (67-97g) — sustained energy

---

## Sample Daily Meal Structure

### Breakfast (600-700 cal)
- Oatmeal (80g dry) with banana and honey
- 2-3 eggs or Greek yogurt
- Toast with almond butter
- Orange juice

### Mid-Morning Snack (300-400 cal)
- Energy bar or granola
- Fruit (apple, dates, or berries)
- Sports drink or electrolyte beverage

### Lunch (800-900 cal)
- Lean protein (chicken, fish, or lean beef): 150g
- Pasta, rice, or sweet potato: 200g cooked
- Vegetables (olive oil drizzle)
- Whole grain bread

### Pre-Workout Snack (200-300 cal)
- Banana with peanut butter
- Sports drink

### Dinner (800-900 cal)
- Protein source: 150g
- Complex carbs: 200g
- Vegetables and healthy fat

### Evening Snack (200-300 cal)
- Greek yogurt, granola, or banana

---

## Hydration Strategy
- **Daily**: 3-4 liters water minimum
- **During runs >90 min**: Sports drink (6-8% carbs)
- **Post-run**: 500ml chocolate milk or electrolyte drink

## Key Timing Tips
- Carb-load 3-4 days before long runs
- Eat 2-3 hours before training
- Refuel within 30 minutes post-workout
